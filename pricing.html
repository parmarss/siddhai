Frequently Asked Questions

Which courts do these plans cover today?
We currently cover only the Supreme Court of India matters. We'll add more courts over time.
What do you mean by "1 case" in the 30-Day Case Pass?
It refers to a specific legal matter type you are researching. Once selected, you can compare lawyers relevant to that matter.
What do I get in the 7-Day Free Preview?
You get access to platform analytics and filters, but lawyer names are masked (hidden) and detailed case lists are locked.
Do you take commissions from lawyers?
No. We are completely independent and do not take commissions or fees from lawyers to list or recommend them.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are generally not available once a plan is active, as data access is immediate. Please check our Terms of Service for details.
