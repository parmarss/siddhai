2. Definitions

  • (a) “Personal Data” means any information that relates to an identified or identifiable individual.
  • (b) “Customer Content” means documents, text, and other content you upload, submit, or store on the Platform (including saved cases and workspace data).
  • (c) “Public Records” means court judgments, orders, listings, and related records obtained from official government or court sources.
  • (d) “De-identified / Aggregated Data” means data that has been processed to remove direct identifiers and combined such that it cannot reasonably be used to identify an individual.

3. What Lawraj does

Lawraj provides tools that transform complex judicial proceedings into easier-to-understand indicators and insights for users, based on publicly available records and information you provide. The Platform may use automated systems (including AI) to summarise, classify, extract fields, and recommend content.

4. Eligibility

The Platform is intended for users who are 18 years or older. We do not knowingly allow children to use the Platform.

5. Information we collect

We collect information in the following categories:

A. Information you provide to us

  • Account and profile information: name, email address, phone number.
  • Corporate billing information: GSTIN and billing address (where applicable).
  • Lawyer profile verification: bar enrollment details if you choose to verify a lawyer profile.
  • Support and communications: messages submitted through chat/support tickets. Calls may be recorded where indicated.
  • Content you upload or submit: case record proceedings, notices, and documents (up to 5MB per document), along with metadata.
  • Searches and inputs: queries, filters, and other inputs you type into the Platform.
  • Workspace data: saved cases and organisational data created within the Platform.

B. Information collected automatically

  • Device and usage data: IP address, device identifiers, browser type, operating system.
  • Usage analytics: pages viewed, features used, clicks, time spent.
  • Approximate location: derived from IP address.
  • Cookies: essential cookies and, subject to your choices, analytics and advertising cookies.

C. Sensitive information

We do not intentionally request sensitive personal data (such as biometric data, Aadhaar numbers, or health information). However, documents you upload or public court records may contain sensitive or special-category information.

Important: Do not upload information protected by attorney-client privilege unless you have the right to share it. Do not upload content that is unlawful to publish, including information identifying sexual offence victims or juveniles.

We do not undertake a general obligation to monitor all uploads, but we may take action (redaction, restriction) if we become aware of unlawful content.

6. How we use your information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • Provide and operate the Platform (authentication, core functionality).
  • Process payments and invoicing.
  • Customer support.
  • Analytics and product improvement.
  • Security and fraud prevention.
  • Legal compliance.
  • Personalisation and recommendations.
  • Marketing (subject to your opt-out rights).
  • AI-enabled features (summarisation, extraction, etc.).

7. Legal basis / consent

We process personal data in accordance with applicable Indian law. Processing is based on your consent, performance of a contract (providing the service), compliance with law, or legitimate operational needs.

8. Marketing choices

We may send promotional messages via email and WhatsApp. You can opt out at any time by using unsubscribe links, in-product settings, or contacting privacy@siddh.ai. Opting out does not affect essential service messages (e.g., OTPs).

9. Cookies and tracking technologies

We use cookies for essential functions, analytics, and advertising. You can manage preferences through browser settings. We respect "Do Not Track" signals where consistent, but primarily rely on cookie controls.

10. Public records and data sourced from government websites

We ingest public court judgments and orders from official sources. Lawraj indexes these records but does not control their content.

If you request redaction or removal of a public record, we may: (a) correct factual errors; (b) limit display or de-index; (c) comply with valid court orders; or (d) apply technical measures like search engine de-indexing. We encourage applicants to provide relevant court orders.

11. AI, model training, and service providers

A. AI features: We use automated systems (AI) for features like summarisation and classification.

B. Use of your content for training:

  • Consumer users: We do not use your uploaded documents to train models by default. Any future training program will be opt-in.
  • Enterprise customers: We do not use Customer Content to train our models.

C. Third-party AI providers: Where available, we instruct providers not to use customer content to train their general models.

12. How we share information

We do not sell personal data. We share information with:

  • Service providers: Payment processors (Razorpay/Stripe), cloud hosting (AWS/GCP/Azure), analytics (Google/Mixpanel), support tools, and AI vendors.
  • Legal and safety: To comply with law or enforce terms.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition.

13. Data storage, location, and transfers

Your information is primarily stored in India. We do not currently store backups outside India.

14. Data retention

We retain data only as long as necessary.

  • Account data: Up to 7 years after closure.
  • Billing records: Up to 8 years.
  • Uploaded documents: Typically up to 90 days after account closure.

15. Account deletion and content controls

You may request account deletion by emailing privacy@siddh.ai. We may perform a soft deletion (deactivation) or hard deletion depending on legal requirements and feasibility.

16. Security

We use encryption in transit (TLS), access controls, and logging. While we implement reasonable safeguards, no system is completely secure.

17. Your rights and choices

Subject to law, you may request to access data, correct inaccuracies, delete data, withdraw consent, or raise a grievance. Contact sparmar@lawraj.com to exercise these rights.

18. Third-party services

The Platform uses third-party services (payments, analytics) governed by their own policies.
